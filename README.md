# FoodHub Order Analysis

## Overview
Exploratory data analysis of FoodHub’s food delivery orders to understand restaurant demand, cuisine popularity, delivery performance, and customer behavior. The insights help improve customer experience and business decisions.

---

## Objective
Analyze order data to:
- Identify popular restaurants and cuisines
- Evaluate delivery and preparation times
- Understand customer ratings and spending patterns
- Estimate revenue generated by the platform

---

## Dataset
- 1,898 food orders
- 9 features including restaurant, cuisine, cost, ratings, preparation time, and delivery time

---

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas, NumPy
- Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Key Insights
- **Top restaurants**: Shake Shack, The Meatball Shop, Blue Ribbon Sushi
- **Most popular weekend cuisine**: American
- **Orders > $20**: ~29%
- **Unrated orders**: 736
- **Mean delivery time**: ~24 minutes
- **Weekend deliveries** are faster than weekdays
- **Orders > 60 minutes (prep + delivery)**: ~10.5%

---

## Revenue Analysis
- 25% commission on orders > $20
- 15% commission on orders > $5  
**Total revenue generated**: ~$6,166

---

## Recommendations
- Optimize weekday delivery operations
- Promote high-rated and high-demand restaurants
- Use ratings and order frequency for targeted promotions
